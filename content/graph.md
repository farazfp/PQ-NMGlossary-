# Knowledge Graph

This is a fullscreen view of all the connections between notes in this digital garden. Use the controls to:

- **Drag** to pan around
- **Scroll** to zoom in/out  
- **Click** nodes to navigate to pages
- **Toggle** between local and global view using the icon

The graph shows the relationships between all your notes, helping you discover unexpected connections and navigate your knowledge base visually.
